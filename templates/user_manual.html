Keys for Handling CUBY.AI

SI.NO COMMANDS FUNCTIONS
1 Hey CUBY To start CUBY
2 Time For time
3 Date For date
4 [Any questioning words] … (or) Questions To define the answer
5 Write … long note … For Generating Long answers in Notepad
6 Write… short… For Generating short answer on Notepad
7 Write… program ... To Generate programs on Notepad
8 Write and Explain … program … For generating Programs and its Explanations
9 Write … For Generating answers in Notepad
10 Make a search about… To search queries in default browser
11 Log out my pc (Confirmation will be asked by CUBY. Say yes to proceed) To lock computer
12 Shut down my pc (Confirmation will be asked by CUBY. Say yes to proceed) To shut down computer
13 Restart my pc (Confirmation will be asked by CUBY. Say yes to proceed) To restart computer
14 Play offline songs To play all songs in playlist
15 Play… To play a specific song
16 …. Remember that To remember some commands at once
17 Do you Remember anything? To repeat the remembered command
18 Screenshot To take screenshot
19 CPU For CPU and battery status
20 WHATSAPP To open WHATSAPP
21 Joke For some technical joke
22 Minimize To minimize screen
23 …YOUTUBE… To play YOUTUBE videos
24 Symbol To convert an image into symbol art
25 Settings To access control panel
26 This pc To explore library and other documents
27 Select all To select all text
28 Save To save a file(renaming has to be done by writer command)
29 Writer For typing
30 Press… To press a single key
31 Go to… To search anything (includes offline and online search)
32 Activate offline To leave the commanding mode
33 Turn off To leave CUBY
